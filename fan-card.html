Welcome to the Official Martin Henderson Fan Club!

Celebrate your love for Martin Henderson, the star of Virgin River, Grey's Anatomy, The Ring, and more. Our Fan Cards provide exclusive perks, updates, and access to fan events. Choose from three membership levels tailored to different levels of fandom.

Membership Levels and Benefits

We offer three tiers of Fan Cards: Bronze, Silver, and Gold. Each level has different fees and escalating benefits. Membership is valid for 24 months from purchase date.

LevelKey BenefitsFee (USD)
Bronze (Entry-level) $3,500
  • Digital membership card (downloadable PDF)
  • Monthly newsletter with news & photos
  • Access to public fan forums
  • 10% discount on official merch
Silver (Dedicated) $7,500
  • Exclusive quarterly video updates from Martin
  • Priority entry to virtual fan meets
  • Signed digital photo
  • 25% merch discount + limited edition sticker pack
Gold $15,000
  • Annual chance to win a signed poster or merch
  • Invites to exclusive live Q&A sessions
  • Physical laminated card shipped worldwide
  • 30% merch discount + personalized shoutout opportunity
  • Free VIP access to select in-person events (where available)
