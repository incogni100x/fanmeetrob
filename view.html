Frequently asked questions

How do I book a meet and greet?
Use the Book Meet & Greet action in the header or above. You’ll be guided through availability, pricing, and confirmation. For general appearances or other requests, use Booking or Contact us.
Is this an official fan experiences channel?
Yes. This site is the verified place to learn about official fan experiences, fan card information, and how to avoid scams. Always confirm links and communications match what you see here.
What is the fan card?
The fan card is an exclusive program for supporters. Details and eligibility are outlined when you apply; processing times and requirements may vary by region.
How can I report a scam or impersonation?
If someone claims to represent official meet and greets outside this channel, do not pay or share personal data. Use the contact options on this site and, when appropriate, report the account to the platform where you saw it.
Who do I contact for support?
Reach out via the Contact us link in the navigation. Include your booking reference or question so the team can respond quickly.
